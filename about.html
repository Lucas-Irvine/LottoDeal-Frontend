Product Description

LottoDeal is a revolutionary new platform that combines the psychological appeal of lotteries with an e-commerce marketplace. Each seller is matched with hundreds of interested micro-bidders who can buy virtual “lottery tickets” for any item. To do so, just press the $1.00, $2.00, or $5.00 bid button on any item, which will contribute that amount to an item’s goal. When the item reaches its goal, it is lotteried off, where each bidder has a chance of winning the item proportional to the amount that they bid. This helps buyers bid on items they desire, but don’t necessarily need to win, at a fraction of the cost. On the other hand, sellers utilize economies of scale to sell their item as fast as possible.
